DATA & ANALYTICS

Use the Paystack dashboard to manage your customers, payments, and track your growth

Trend Graphs

Your dashboard home screen shows your trend of revenue. You can filter over a period of time to see how your business has been performing.

View and export all your data

Your customer, transactions, pages, plans, and settings are listed on your dashboard. Each section lets you view, manage and export the data in csv format.

Multi-Business

Sometimes, jack of all trades. Use one Paystack account to create as many businesses as you need, and easily switch between them on your dashboard.

Choose your payout schedule

Use your dashboard to set your settlement preference. You can choose between the default next-day settlement or opt to manually trigger a settlement.

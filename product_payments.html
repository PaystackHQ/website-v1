HOSTED PAYMENT PAGES

Payment pages are the easiest way to accept payments for your product or service. No code required.

Quickly implement campaigns for your products, services and subscription plans without writing code. Create a payment page from the dashboard and customize it as much as you want.

  • Use it for one time payments or subscriptions

  • Set fixed or variable payment amounts

  • Collect as much extra information as you need

  • Create memorable links using custom URLs

  • Optional thank you message or redirect after payment
